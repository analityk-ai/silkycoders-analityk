# EduFlow Next Stakeholder Meeting Agenda

**Goal:** Clarify MVP scope, key decisions, and open questions from last meeting.
**Proposed duration:** 60 minutes

## 1. Opening and outcomes (5 min)
- Confirm attendees and roles for decisions
- Review agenda and desired decisions for today

## 2. Recap of current scope and constraints (5 min)
- Key takeaways from Jan 10 notes
- Timeline, team capacity, and launch expectations

## 3. MVP scope and priorities (15 min)
- Must-have vs phase-2 features
- Confirm success criteria for v1

## 4. Roles, access, and user journeys (10 min)
- Role definitions and permissions
- Guest access decision for v1
- Core journeys: student, instructor, admin

## 5. Content and learning experience (10 min)
- Content types, quizzes, and optional features
- Publishing workflow and quality standards
- Progress, certificates, ratings, and forums

## 6. Monetization and policy decisions (5 min)
- Pricing model, free trial, refund rules
- Promo/discount strategy

## 7. Technical and integration decisions (5 min)
- Hosting, video delivery, and performance targets
- External integrations (email, analytics, payment, SSO, LMS)

## 8. Compliance, risk, and security (3 min)
- GDPR, PCI, accessibility, data protection

## 9. Decisions, owners, and next steps (2 min)
- Decision log
- Action items and due dates
